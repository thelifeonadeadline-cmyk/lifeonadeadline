Heyyyy there...

My name is Nyasha Unami Zuva, and I’m the founder of Life On A Deadline — a digital space dedicated to helping students stay productive, organized, and inspired. As a student balancing academics, personal goals, and everyday responsibilities, I understand how overwhelming life can get. That’s why I created this platform: to offer tools and support for managing your time, building self-discipline, and staying focused — even when deadlines pile up.

Vision for Life On A Deadline

My long-term goal is to grow Life On A Deadline into a go-to platform that empowers students around the world to take control of their lives through smart systems and self-management tools. I hope to expand the site with more interactive resources, collaborate with experts, and eventually turn it into a community that makes productivity feel less lonely and more achievable.
